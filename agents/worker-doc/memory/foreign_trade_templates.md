# 外贸邮件话术与商务模板

## 首次接触邮件

### 开发信模板 (Cold Email)
```
Subject: {Product Name} Supplier from China - {Company Name}

Dear {Recipient Name},

I hope this email finds you well.

This is {Your Name} from {Company Name}, a professional manufacturer of {Product Category} in China with {X} years of experience.

I noticed your company dealing with {related products} and believe our products might be of interest to you.

**Our Advantages:**
- {Advantage 1}
- {Advantage 2}
- {Advantage 3}

Could you let me know what products you're currently sourcing? I'd be happy to send you our catalog and competitive quotes.

Best regards,
{Your Name}
{Company Name}
{Phone Number}
```

### 回复询盘 (Reply to Inquiry)
```
Subject: Re: {Original Subject}

Dear {Customer Name},

Thank you for your inquiry about {product name}.

I've attached our product specification sheet and FOB price list for your reference.

**Product Details:**
- Item No.: {XXX}
- Material: {XXX}
- MOQ: {XXX} pcs
- Lead Time: {X} weeks
- Payment Terms: {T/T 30% deposit, 70% against B/L copy}

Please let me know if you need samples or have any questions.

Best regards,
{Your Name}
```

## 报价阶段

### 产品报价单 (Quotation)
```
Subject: Product Quotation - {Product Name} - Valid for 30 days

Dear {Customer Name},

Thank you for your inquiry. Please find our quotation as follows:

**QUOTATION**
Date: {Date}
Quote No.: {QUO-XXXX}

| Item No. | Description | Qty | Unit Price | Total |
|----------|-------------|-----|------------|-------|
| P001     | {Product}   | 500 | USD 10.00  | 5000  |

- **FOB Port**: {Port Name}
- **Payment**: T/T 30% deposit, 70% against B/L copy
- **Lead Time**: {X} weeks after deposit
- **Validity**: 30 days

Please feel free to contact us if you have any questions.

Best regards,
{Your Name}
```

### 讨价还价 (Negotiation)
```
Dear {Customer},

Thank you for your feedback regarding our quotation.

After internal discussion, we're pleased to offer the following adjustments:

1. Unit price reduced by 5% → **USD {New Price}**
2. Free samples provided (value USD {Amount})
3. Priority production schedule for your order

This special offer is valid for the next 7 days.

Looking forward to your order!

Best regards,
{Your Name}
```

## 售后服务

### 货物出运通知 (Shipment Advice)
```
Subject: Your Order {Order No.} Has Been Shipped

Dear {Customer},

We're pleased to inform you that your order has been dispatched.

**Shipment Details:**
- Order No.: {XXX}
- BL No.: {XXX}
- Vessel: {Vessel Name}
- ETD: {Date}
- ETA: {Date}
- Port of Loading: {Port}
- Port of Destination: {Port}

Documents attached:
1. Bill of Lading (B/L)
2. Commercial Invoice
3. Packing List
4. Certificate of Origin

Please don't hesitate to contact us if you have any questions.

Best regards,
{Your Name}
```

## 常用商务短语

### 礼貌用语
- "I hope this email finds you well."
- "Thank you for your prompt response."
- "I appreciate your patience."
- "Please feel free to contact us."

### 专业表达
- "Our MOQ is..."
- "The lead time is..."
- "We accept payment by T/T."
- "The price is FOB..."
- "Please find attached..."

### 跟进用语
- "I hope you have had a chance to review..."
- "I'm following up on my previous email..."
- "Just a gentle reminder..."
- "I'm looking forward to hearing from you."
